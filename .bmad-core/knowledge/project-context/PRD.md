## ๐ฏ Core Functionality

### Core Functionality (ฺฉ ูพุงุฑุงฺฏุฑุงู)

Play and Pay is a blockchain-powered micro-payment SaaS platform that enables real-time, consumption-based payments for digital content. Our core functionality revolves around a seamless payment gateway that connects to content platforms (OTT/VOD, music, podcasts, e-learning, etc.) and automatically calculates usage every few seconds or minutes, deducting from users' wallets in real-time. Users can fund their wallets with fiat or cryptocurrency, and pay only for the exact minutes they consume contentโeliminating subscription fatigue and intrusive ads. For businesses, we provide a simple API for integration, a comprehensive dashboard for analytics and revenue management, and automated settlement tools. The entire system is built on Algorand blockchain for ultra-low transaction fees and instant confirmations, ensuring a fair, transparent, and efficient payment ecosystem where every second of consumption is fairly compensated.
